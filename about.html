ABOUT

This project is a gallery that serves as a quick guide to the members and their music. It’s dedicated to my favorite K-pop group, who stayed with me throughout my teenage years. Their songs made me feel understood and reminded me that I wasn’t alone in what I felt growing up. Their music has continued to be a comforting part of my adult life, and this gallery is a small tribute to the impact they’ve had on me.
